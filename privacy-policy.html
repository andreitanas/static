Privacy Policy for Simple Budget Calculator

Last Updated: August 6, 2025

Overview

Simple Budget Calculator is designed with privacy at its core. We believe your financial data should remain completely private and under your control.

Data Collection

We collect NO data whatsoever. Simple Budget Calculator:

  • Does not collect any personal information
  • Does not collect any financial data
  • Does not collect any usage analytics
  • Does not collect any crash reports
  • Does not require account creation or login

Data Storage

All your financial data is stored locally on your device only using SQLite database technology. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • We have no access to your information
  • Your data is not transmitted over the internet
  • No cloud storage or backup services are used
  • Only you can access your financial information

Third-Party Services

Simple Budget Calculator does not integrate with any third-party services:

  • No analytics services (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting services
  • No cloud storage providers
  • No social media integrations

Network Access

Simple Budget Calculator does not require internet connection and makes no network requests:

  • No data is sent to any servers
  • No data is received from any servers
  • The app works completely offline
  • No tracking or monitoring occurs

Children's Privacy

Since we collect no data at all, Simple Budget Calculator is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices (which is highly unlikely given our local-only approach), we will update this policy and notify users through an app update.

Contact Information

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:
simple.budget@tanas.ca

Your Rights

Since we collect no data:

  • There is no data to delete
  • There is no data to export
  • There is no data to modify
  • Your privacy is automatically protected
