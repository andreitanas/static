Simple Budget Calculator - Support

Simple Budget Calculator is designed to be intuitive and easy to use. Most questions can be answered by exploring the app, but we're here to help!

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

When you first open the app, you'll see a guided setup process. Simply follow the 3 steps:

  1. Add your bank accounts
  2. Add your income sources
  3. Add your expenses and bills
Where is my data stored?

All your financial data is stored locally on your device only. We don't collect, transmit, or store any of your information on our servers. Your privacy is completely protected.

How do I backup my data?

Currently, all data is stored locally on your device. We recommend using iTunes/Finder backup or iCloud device backup to preserve your app data when switching devices.

Can I sync between devices?

The current version stores data locally only. Cloud sync features may be added in future updates based on user feedback.

What frequency options are available?

You can set income and expenses to occur:

  • Weekly
  • Bi-weekly
  • Monthly
  • Quarterly
  • Semi-annual
  • Yearly
  • One-time
How accurate are the balance forecasts?

The forecasts are based on your input data and recurring patterns. They provide estimates to help with planning, but actual results may vary based on real-world changes to your income and expenses.

How do I delete my data?

To clear all data, you can delete and reinstall the app. Since all data is stored locally, this will completely remove all your financial information.

Technical Requirements

  • iOS Version: Requires iOS 16.0 or later
  • Device: Compatible with iPhone and iPad
  • Storage: Minimal storage required (under 50MB)
  • Internet: No internet connection required
